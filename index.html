Common Questions

Does Knot book the Uber for me?

No. Knot matches you with other travelers. Once you meet, one person uses their own Uber/Lyft app to request the ride, and the others pay them back (usually via Venmo/CashApp).

Is it safe?

Safety is our priority. We use identity verification and university email matching. However, you should always exercise the same caution you would when meeting anyone in public.

Is my location data private?

Yes. Your exact location is never shown on a public map. We only use relative distance to find matches.
